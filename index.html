About

A short version.

I'm a product manager with a soft spot for early-stage ambiguity — the part where the brief is half-written and the data is half-clean. Previously co-founded LANS, a workspace-matching startup in San Francisco, and led growth product work at fast-delivery and CPG startups through hypergrowth.

Today I'm finishing my master's in Data Science & Decision Analytics at Cornell Tech, where I lead product on Mileo and previously ran an AI-diagnostics initiative at UC Berkeley Lab. After graduation I'm joining Arista Networks as a Product Automation Manager — moving into hardware and the systems side of networking infrastructure.

Outside of work: oil painting, piano, and long walks looking for the one good coffee in any new city.
